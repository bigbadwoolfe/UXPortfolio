Employment, education and the rest

For the better part of the last year I have been a Team Lead and a Front End Development Tech Lead for TheRedPin Inc, a technology start-up in Toronto.

My daily tasks are mainly consisted of overlooking and participating in the development and UX design of the company's main web site, which is a complex web application with many thousands of daily users. I am also mentoring a team of developers and UX designers on development and UX best practises daily.

Before that I was the UX Lead Designer and Web Developer at Seagate Technology. Here I led major redesign efforts for several products and designed the user interface for dozens of features that were integrated with already existing products. I also led the design of two brand new web products and an online store for the company. When I was not involved in hands-on design, I overlooked the UX on several projects and mentored less experienced developers and designers, as well as created, maintained and enforced UI standards for the products I had designed.

My specialty are responsive user interfaces for web applications. I hold a degree in computer science from University of Toronto. I also spend hours each week educating myself and keeping up to date with the current usability principles and design trends.

When I'm not working, I'm likely spending time with my wife and 4 year old daughter, or I'm preparing for my next archery tournament.
